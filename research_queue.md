## How to Write a Good Hypothesis

Each entry must have:
- **Question**: A testable yes/no question about a specific change
- **Prior**: Numerical probability (0-100%) that this improves the metric, with reasoning
- **Mechanism**: WHY would this work? Causal story from domain science or ML theory
- **Source**: Which paper/finding motivated this hypothesis
- **Impact**: HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW based on expected metric improvement and breadth of effect
