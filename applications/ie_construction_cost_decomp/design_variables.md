# Design Variables: Construction Cost Decomposition (Option C)

This is an Option C (decomposition) project. The "design variables" are the components being ablated/decomposed rather than optimization parameters.

## Components Under Decomposition

### Material Categories (from WPM28)
1. Concrete cluster: cement (611), ready-mixed concrete (621), concrete blocks (631), precast (64262)
2. Steel cluster: structural steel (651, 65161, 65162), reinforcing metal (652), other steel (653)
3. Timber cluster: rough softwood (66161), hardwood (66162), treated/machined (67161), windows/doors (67162)
4. Insulation (702)
5. HVAC/mechanical (701)
6. Electrical (691, 69162, 69163)
7. Plumbing (700)
8. Glass (706)
9. Other: sand/gravel (60161, 60162), bituminous (681, 682), pipes (703, 70371, 70372), plaster (704), paints (705), metal fittings (708), other (709)

### Cost Component Split
1. Hard costs (53%): materials (25%), labour (25%), site works (3%)
2. Soft costs (47%): land, fees, finance, VAT, Part V, dev levies, margin, BCAR

### Trade-Level Decomposition
1. Substructure (8%)
2. Frame/Upper Floors (12%)
3. Roof (7%)
4. External Walls/Cladding (10%)
5. Windows/External Doors (6%)
6. Internal Walls/Partitions (4%)
7. Internal Finishes (8%)
8. Fittings/Fixtures (5%)
9. Mechanical Services (14%)
10. Electrical Services (10%)
11. Stairs (2%)
12. Preliminaries (10%)
13. External/Site Works (4%)

### Shock Regimes
1. Pre-COVID steady state (2015-2019)
2. COVID disruption (2020-2021)
3. Ukraine/energy crisis (2022-2023)
4. Post-crisis normalization (2024-2025)

### Regulatory Regimes
1. Pre-NZEB (before Nov 2019)
2. Post-NZEB (Nov 2019+)
3. BCAR implementation (2014+)
