# Feature Candidates: Warp Drive Physics

## Metric Properties (per warp solution)

1. **Superluminal capability** — Whether v_s > c is achievable (binary)
2. **Eulerian energy density sign** — Positive, negative, or mixed
3. **NEC status** — Satisfied/violated (determines physical realisability)
4. **WEC status** — Satisfied/violated
5. **SEC status** — Satisfied/violated
6. **DEC status** — Satisfied/violated
7. **ADM mass** — Zero (Natario class) or positive (shell-based)
8. **Exotic matter mass** — Total negative energy in solar masses
9. **Shape function family** — Top-hat, Van Den Broeck, Lentz soliton, shell
10. **Lapse function** — Unit (Natario class) or non-unit (shell solutions)
11. **Spatial metric** — Flat (delta_ij) or non-flat
12. **Expansion theta** — Zero (Natario) or nonzero (Alcubierre)
13. **Event horizons** — Present (superluminal) or absent (subluminal)
14. **CTC potential** — Creates closed timelike curves or not
15. **Time delay delta_t** — Lense-Thirring-like measurable effect

## Computational Proxies

16. **v_s / c ratio** — Primary determinant of energy requirements
17. **M_exotic / M_sun** — Normalised exotic matter mass
18. **R * sigma** — Dimensionless bubble sharpness
19. **M_ADM / M_shell** — Fraction of mass contributing to ADM
20. **max(|p_i|/rho)** — Ratio controlling DEC violation threshold
21. **QI bound ratio** — Ford-Roman quantum inequality bound vs actual negative energy
22. **Casimir-to-warp ratio** — Available Casimir energy / required warp energy
