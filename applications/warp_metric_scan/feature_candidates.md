# Feature Candidates

## Geometric Features (computed from metric)

1. **G_00 (Einstein tensor time-time component)**: Primary diagnostic for WEC. Negative = exotic matter required.
2. **G_00 + G_11 (NEC combination)**: Primary diagnostic for NEC along x-direction.
3. **Ricci scalar R**: Trace of Ricci tensor; drives f(R) corrections.
4. **R^2**: Square of Ricci scalar; the leading f(R) correction term.
5. **R_00 (Ricci tensor time-time)**: Needed for f(R) effective energy density.
6. **K_ij (extrinsic curvature)**: Controls expansion/contraction; drives energy density.
7. **Theta (expansion scalar)**: v_s * (x_s/r_s) * df/dr_s; positive behind, negative ahead.
8. **Sigma_ij (shear tensor)**: Trace-free part of K_ij; contributes to energy conditions.

## Framework-Specific Features

9. **Phi(x,y,z) (KK scalar field)**: Extra-dimension modulation; phi = 1 + alpha * f.
10. **G_00^(5D) - G_00^(4D)**: KK contribution to effective 4D energy density.
11. **G_44 (extra-dimensional Einstein component)**: Equation of motion for phi.
12. **Delta_G00_fR**: f(R) geometric correction = 2*alpha_R2*(R*R_00 - R^2*g_00/4).
13. **H_00(S)**: Torsion contribution to effective Einstein equations in EC theory.
14. **S^lambda_mu_nu**: Torsion tensor from spin density.
15. **s_0 * |nabla f|**: Spin density concentrated at bubble wall gradient.
16. **E_mu_nu**: Projected bulk Weyl tensor on brane.
17. **pi_mu_nu**: Quadratic stress-energy correction on brane.
18. **C_W * (warp curvature profile)**: Dark radiation contribution from bulk geometry.

## Physical Constraints

19. **Solar system KK constraint**: alpha must be compatible with Gravity Probe B limits.
20. **f(R) Dolgov-Kawasaki stability**: f''(R) > 0 required for stability.
21. **Spin density physical limit**: s_0 must correspond to achievable angular momentum.
22. **Brane tension constraint**: lambda > (1 TeV)^4 from collider bounds.
