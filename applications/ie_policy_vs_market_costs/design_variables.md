# Design Variables

These are the policy levers that government can adjust, with their current values and plausible ranges.

| Variable | Current Value | Min Plausible | Max Plausible | International Precedent |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| VAT rate on new residential | 13.5% | 0% (UK zero-rating) | 23% (standard rate) | UK=0%, FR=10%, DE=19% |
| Part V rate | 20% of units | 0% (abolished) | 30% (some advocate) | UK s.106 ~15-35% negotiable |
| Dev contributions (Dublin) | EUR 25,000 | EUR 0 (waived) | EUR 35,000 | UK CIL varies widely |
| Dev contributions (rural) | EUR 6,000-10,000 | EUR 0 | EUR 15,000 | Often waived for rural |
| BCAR compliance cost | EUR 4,500-8,000 | EUR 0 (self-cert, pre-2014) | EUR 10,000 | UK building control ~EUR 2,000 |
| Planning fees | EUR 65-1,500 | EUR 0 | EUR 5,000 | Trivially small either way |
| Developer margin | 15-20% | 10% (compressed) | 25% (high risk) | UK=15-20%, DE=10-15% |
| Finance rate | 7-10% | 2% (state finance) | 12% (high risk) | HFA lending ~3-4% |
| Land cost | Market price | Agricultural (EUR 10-20k/ha via CPO) | Current (EUR 170k-1.6M/ha) | LDA mandate |
| Build cost (modular) | Baseline | -20% (modular) | -30% (advanced modular) | Nordic experience |
