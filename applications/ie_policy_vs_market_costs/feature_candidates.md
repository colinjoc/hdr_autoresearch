# Feature Candidates / Cost Components

## Policy-Set Components (Government Can Change)

| Component | Typical Value | Data Source | Mechanism |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| VAT on new residential | 13.5% of construction + professional fees | Revenue Commissioners | Statutory rate; applied to hard costs + professional fees |
| Part V obligation | 20% of units at cost price | Planning & Development Act 2000 s.96 | Cross-subsidy; effective cost = gap between cost and market price |
| Development contributions | EUR 6k-25k per unit | Each LA's development contribution scheme | Infrastructure levy; set by elected councillors |
| BCAR compliance | EUR 4.5k-8k per unit | RIAI/SCSI estimates | Assigned certifier + inspection regime |
| Planning application fees | EUR 65-1,500 per unit | SI 600/2001 | Statutory fee; includes fire cert + disability cert |

## Market-Driven Components (Government Cannot Directly Change)

| Component | Typical Value | Data Source | Mechanism |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| Materials | ~45% of hard cost | CSO WPM28 | Global commodity prices; local supply chain |
| Labour | ~40% of hard cost | CSO EHQ03 | Wage rates; labour shortage dynamics |
| Site works | ~15% of hard cost | Buildcost.ie | Substructure, drainage, external works |
| Land | EUR 5k-45k per unit | CSO RZLPA02 | Market price for zoned residential land |
| Professional fees | 10-15% of hard cost | RIAI/Engineers Ireland | Architect, engineer, quantity surveyor |
| Finance | 7-10% of development cost | Commercial bank rates | Interest on drawdown during construction |
| Developer margin | 15-20% of GDV | SCSI viability benchmark | Required return to make development commercially attractive |

## Hybrid / Debatable Classification

| Component | Current Classification | Argument for Other Side |
|:---|:---|:---|
| Land | MARKET | Government could CPO at agricultural price; RZLT penalises hoarding |
| Developer margin | MARKET | Government could accept lower margins through direct delivery |
| Finance | MARKET | Government could provide low-cost development finance (HFA) |
| Professional fees | MARKET | Fee scales partially regulated; RIAI sets guidance rates |
