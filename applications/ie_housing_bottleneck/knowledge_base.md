# Knowledge Base: Irish Housing Bottleneck Synthesis

## Established Facts from Predecessor Studies

### Pipeline Parameters (measured)
- **Permission volume**: 32,000-43,000 units/yr (BHQ15, 2019-2025). No upward trend. Median ~38,000.
- **Lapse rate**: 9.5% (NRU>0 2017-2019), cluster-bootstrap CI 4.4-15.6% (PL-4).
- **Permission-to-commencement duration**: median 232 days, IQR 97-550 days (PL-1).
- **Commencement-to-CCC duration**: median 498 days, IQR ~330-720 days (PL-1).
- **Total pipeline latency**: median 962 days = 32 months (PL-1 complete-timeline subcohort).
- **CCC filing rate**: 40.9% of commenced (85,565 projects). Non-opt-out: 59.8% (S-1).
- **Overall pipeline yield**: 35.1% [28-35%] (S-1). Non-opt-out yield: 51.4%.
- **Opt-out share**: 31.6% of commenced cohort. 0% CCC rate by design.
- **ABP decision time**: 18 weeks (2017) -> 42 weeks (2023-2024). SOP 64% -> 25% (PL-3).
- **ABP utilisation ratio**: rho peaked at 1.45 in 2022 (PL-3).
- **SHD JR state-loss rate**: 14/16 = 87.5% (2018-2021) (PL-1 SHD).
- **Direct JR tax**: 105,504 unit-months, range [85,404, 150,204] (S-2).
- **Counterfactual completions gap**: 7,421-16,638 units over 2018-2024 (S-2).
- **LDA delivery**: ca. 850/yr (2023), 100% Project Tosaigh acquisition, 3% of HFA (PL-3 LDA).
- **Actual completions 2024**: 34,177 (NDA12). 2025 annualised: ~35,000.
- **HFA target**: 50,500 completions/yr.

### Key Findings
1. Permission volume is the binding constraint if yield > ~70%. At 35.1% yield, 144k permissions needed. At 51.4% (non-opt-out), 98k needed.
2. The CCC non-filing rate is primarily a regulatory artefact (opt-out), not genuine non-completion.
3. ABP slowdown was within-case-type productivity, not mix shift (Oaxaca-Blinder).
4. JR contribution to ABP slowdown cannot be separated from capacity crisis at n=10 annual observations.
5. LDA is an attribution share, not additionality. Zero additional homes built.
6. Construction-sector capacity appears to be ~35,000/yr based on 2024 peak.

### Known Pitfalls
- Do NOT cite the all-cohort lapse rate (27.4%) — it is inflated by join failure.
- Do NOT cite 144k permissions needed without noting the opt-out adjustment (98k at non-opt-out yield).
- ABP rho is an accounting identity, not a causal explanation.
- The JR indirect channel is bounded [0, 9,305] unit-months with no identified point within that range.
- LDA "share of completions" is attribution, not additionality.
