## Discovery space (Phase B)

The Phase B projection explores the successor regime's potential to reduce JR rate further. Design variables:

- **DV_A** Target JR-rate-per-permission post-PDA-2024 — scenario values 1% (pre-SHD baseline), 2%, 5%, SHD level (~20%).
- **DV_B** Annual LRD+Housing scheme pipeline — scenario values 200, 300, 400 schemes/year (housing crisis scaling).
- **DV_C** ELCFA uptake rate — scenario values 25%, 50%, 75% (reduced lodgement pressure).
- **DV_D** Planning and Environment List throughput — scenario values 1×, 2×, 3× 2023 baseline.
- **DV_E** Implementation lag of PDA 2024 provisions — 0, 12, 24 months.

## Non-design variables (fixed by context / not alterable in scope)

- EU law grounds (EIA, Habitats, Aarhus) — not policy-variable.
- Supreme Court case law on reasons doctrine (Connelly, Balz) — common-law evolution.
- CJEU rulings on costs — outside Ireland's unilateral control.
- Housing crisis macro drivers — outside planning-law scope.
