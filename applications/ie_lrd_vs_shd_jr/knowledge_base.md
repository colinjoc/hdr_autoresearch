# Knowledge base — LRD vs SHD JR

## Established facts (from primary ABP/OPR sources)

### Regime timeline
- SHD regime: 3 July 2017 (S.I. 270/2017, 271/2017) → December 2021 (Act 2021 repeal).
- LRD regime: December 2021 → ongoing. Two-stage: LA grant → ABP appeal under s 127 PDA 2000.
- Planning and Development Act 2024: signed 2024, staggered implementation 2025-2026. ELCFA to replace pre-existing costs protection.

### SHD volume (predecessor project + ABP annual reports)
- SHD applications decided: 2017 started; 2020 = 137; 2021 = 113; 2022 = 80 (mostly windown); 2023-2024 = windown.
- SHD applications valid (received): 2020 = 112; 2021 = 118; 2022 = 127 (last-minute lodgement spike); post-2022 = 0.
- SHD permissions granted: roughly 60-75% of decisions in 2018-2021 (predecessor project context).

### LRD volume (ABP annual reports 2022-2024)
- LRD appeals received: 2022 = 6; 2023 = 52; 2024 = 71.
- LRD appeals concluded: 2022 = 1; 2023 = 36; 2024 = 79.
- Cumulative LRD appeals concluded by end-2024 = 116.

## Stylised facts
- Base-rate Irish planning JR (pre-SHD) ≈1% per ABP decision, state-win ≈60%.
- SHD-era JR rate ≈20% (Oneil 2020 estimate) per permission; state-loss rate 85-90% on decided cases.
- UK NSIP analogue: 3-5% JR rate, 25-35% state-loss rate.
- Concession rate has risen in 2024 vs historical: 2021=19 of 54 outcomes (35%), 2024=53 of 129 (41%).
- Court throughput doubled when Planning and Environment List commenced (late 2022).

## Known pitfalls
- **Reporting lag is decisive.** LRD JRs lodged in 2023-2024 are still pending. Decided-JR data for LRD is thin. Conservative interpretation required.
- **SHD carryover.** Many JRs decided in 2023-2024 are against SHD permissions granted in 2019-2021. The year-of-initiation breakdown in ABP Tables 4-5 shows this explicitly. Do not conflate "decided in 2024" with "LRD-era case".
- **Denominator ambiguity.** JR rate can be per permission, per decision (including refusals), per unit, per scheme. Each produces different numbers. Fix denominator and state which.
- **Partial 2024 data.** ABP 2024 report published early 2025 includes end-of-year outcomes not yet classified.
- **Press counts over-count.** Press includes withdrawals and pending; OPR counts decided only.
- **Small-n inference.** LRD side has few data points; use exact tests and Bayesian shrinkage; do not claim precision.
- **Concurrent trends.** Planning and Environment List (Nov 2022), Heather Hill Supreme Court judgment (July 2022), and 2024 Act signal all influence decision patterns in the LRD era.

## Definitional conventions adopted

- **"SHD era"**: ABP decisions dated 3 July 2017 - 16 December 2021.
- **"LRD era"**: ABP appeal decisions dated 17 December 2021 onwards.
- **"JR rate per permission"**: number of permissions JR'd / number of permissions granted in that regime-year cohort.
- **"State-loss rate"**: (quashed + conceded) / (total with substantive outcome). Withdrawn excluded unless noted.
- **"Substantive outcome"**: won, lost (quashed), conceded, or withdrawn — excludes procedural interim judgments.
- **Reporting-lag flag**: any cohort with <24 months since permission-grant is "partial" and labelled accordingly.
