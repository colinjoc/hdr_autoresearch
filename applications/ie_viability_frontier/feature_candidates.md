# Feature Candidates — Development Viability

## Price-Side Features
1. **Median sale price by county** — from CSO PPR/HPM09, primary GDV determinant
2. **RPPI level and 12-month change** — price trajectory captures momentum
3. **Price-to-income ratio** — affordability ceiling from Central Bank rules
4. **New vs secondhand price premium** — new homes command premium varying by county
5. **Apartment vs house price differential** — apartment viability differs structurally

## Cost-Side Features
6. **Construction cost per sqm by region** — from Buildcost.ie, primary hard cost
7. **BEA04 construction production index** — cost trajectory over time
8. **SCSI Tender Price Index change** — forward-looking cost inflation
9. **Development contributions per unit** — Section 48/49 charges by LA
10. **Part V effective cost per market unit** — cross-subsidy burden
11. **Finance rate** — development finance cost of capital
12. **Build duration** — time-in-money exposure
13. **Professional fees as % of construction** — typically 10-15%

## Land-Side Features
14. **Median land price per hectare (RZLPA02)** — county-level land cost
15. **Land cost per unit at assumed density** — land/density interaction
16. **Density (units per hectare)** — planning-permitted density varies by location
17. **RZLT liability** — 3% annual charge on undeveloped zoned land value

## Viability Metrics
18. **Residual land value** — GDV minus all costs except land
19. **Viability margin (% of sale price)** — (sale price - total cost) / sale price
20. **Viability class** — viable (>5%), marginal (-5% to 5%), unviable (<-5%)
21. **Break-even sale price** — price needed for zero margin at actual costs
22. **Cost-to-price ratio** — total cost / sale price (Tobin's Q inverse)
23. **Land share of total cost** — land cost as % of total development cost

## Spatial/Structural Features
24. **Region (NUTS3)** — regional grouping
25. **Distance to Dublin** — proxy for market depth
26. **Urban/rural classification** — CSO settlement classification
27. **Zoned hectares in county** — scale of available land
28. **Application intensity (from U-1)** — apps/ha/yr as activity indicator
29. **ESB connections (NDQ04)** — completion proxy
