# Design Variables: IE Zoned Land Conversion

This is an Option C (decomposition) project — we reverse-engineer why zoned land is not converting to planning applications, rather than optimising a model.

## Primary Outcome Variable
- **Application intensity**: residential permission applications per hectare of zoned land per year (apps/ha/yr)

## Design Variables (levers that could change the conversion rate)

| # | Variable | Current Value | Mechanism | Actionable? |
|---|----------|---------------|-----------|-------------|
| 1 | RZLT rate | 3% of market value/yr | Imposes holding cost, reduces option value of waiting | Yes (policy) |
| 2 | Viability ratio | 1.26 (national) | Sale price / total cost — must exceed ~1.2 for development | Indirectly (subsidies, cost reduction) |
| 3 | Construction cost | EUR ~250k/unit | Hard construction + contributions + fees | Partially (productivity, scale) |
| 4 | Development contributions | EUR 5k-30k/unit | LA-levied infrastructure charge | Yes (policy) |
| 5 | Processing speed | 63 days median | Time from filing to decision | Yes (institutional) |
| 6 | Approval rate | ~88% | Proportion granted of decided | Marginally (clearer policies) |
| 7 | Water infrastructure capacity | Varies by LA | Irish Water connection availability | Yes (capital investment) |
| 8 | Zoning density allowance | Varies by plan | Maximum permissible density | Yes (development plan) |
| 9 | Planning conditions | Varies | Conditions attached to grants | Partially (standardisation) |
| 10 | Interest rates | ECB rate | Cost of development finance | No (exogenous) |
| 11 | Mortgage availability | CBI macro-prudential rules | Demand-side credit constraint | Yes (policy) |
| 12 | Land ownership fragmentation | Unknown | Many small owners → coordination failure | Difficult (CPO powers) |
| 13 | LDA activation | ~850 homes/yr | Public land activation | Yes (scale LDA) |
| 14 | Part V requirement | 20% social/affordable | Cost to developer | Yes (policy) |
| 15 | Help-to-Buy subsidy | EUR 30k max | Demand stimulus for first-time buyers | Yes (policy) |

## Scenario Variables (for Phase B counterfactuals)
- If Fingal intensity rose to East+Midlands average → additional applications
- If RZLT reduces hold-out by X% → implied additional applications
- If viability ratio rose to 1.5 → implied capacity utilization change
- If all regions matched Southern rate → national application total
